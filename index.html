A farmer grows his flock of sheep to 495 and realizes that his sheepdog is ready for retirement, so he goes to a pet store and asks the clerk for the best sheepdog he sells.

"This is the best one," the clerk said. "He speaks four languages, plays chess and recites Shakespeare on command."

"He's right," the dog says. "I'm the best sheepdog there is."

"That's all well and good," says the farmer, "but can you round up 495 sheep every day?"

"I can round up as many sheep as you want," the dog replies.

Impressed, the farmer buys the dog. He takes him to the top of a hill overlooking his pasture to look out over his 495 sheep.

"Alright, round them up," he tells the dog.

The dog looks thoughtfully at the flock for a moment before telling the farmer, "You have 500 sheep."
